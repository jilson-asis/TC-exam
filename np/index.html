Frequently asked questions

We connect people who are looking for house call clinicians, home care,and home health services to local service providers. Seniors, case managers, and social workers use the TrueCare24 platform to find house call doctors, nurse practitioners, caregivers, nurses, therapists, and lab & imaging services at home.
Check the case description, if you would like to take the patient, click "Learn More", and you will receive the phone number that will connect you directly to a patient. Call and talk to a patient and let us know whether you are confirming the visit.
For private pay patients, you set the price during a phone call. If the visit has a fix price and paid by the payor, you can find the rate in the request description before accepting the patient. 
TrueCare24 doesn’t employ or contract network providers. TrueCare24 provides a technology platform that enables users to connect easily with top rated third-party health care medical service providers. You will be providing  medical services as a licensed independent health care practitioner
For private pay clients, you need to have your own established relationship with a supervising physician. In case of request from payors or health organizations, the supervising physician might be provided. It will be separately mentioned in the request. 
Out providers maintain their own malpractice insurance as independent health care practitioners. We are working towards providing all the tools necessary (including insurance) for skilled medical practitioners.
